\section{Cluster-Robust Tests (Supplementary Table S6)}
\label{sec:cluster_robust}

\begin{table}[htbp]
\centering
\caption{Cluster-robust statistical tests using date-cluster bootstrap (1,000 resamples). Event-row p-values in parentheses.}
\label{tab:supp_cluster}
\begin{tabular}{lccccc}
\toprule
Comparison & Metric & Event-Row $p$ & Cluster-Robust $p$ & 95\% CI & Conclusion \\
\midrule
Full Hybrid vs.\ LightGBM (binary) & Macro F1 & $4.16 \times 10^{-5}$ & $8.32 \times 10^{-4}$ & $[+0.012, +0.065]$ & Superior \\
Full Hybrid vs.\ GRU (binary) & Macro F1 & $1.52 \times 10^{-4}$ & $2.87 \times 10^{-3}$ & $[+0.005, +0.058]$ & Superior \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{table}

Cluster-robust versions use date-cluster bootstrap with 1,000 resamples. The 47 date clusters contain 2--89Event rows each (median 14). Cluster-robust $p$-values are qualitatively similar to event-row $p$-values but with wider confidence intervals, confirming the robustness of the superiority claims.
